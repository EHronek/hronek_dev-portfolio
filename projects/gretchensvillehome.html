Gretchensville Home – Hospitality Website

  • Project Type: Client Website / Hospitality Brand
  • Tech Stack: HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, JavaScript
  • Live Website: gretchensvillehome.com

Designed and developed the official website for Gretchensville Home, a boutique homestay offering a warm, elegant, and personalized hospitality experience. The goal was to translate the physical comfort and character of the home into a clean, inviting digital presence.

The website features a modern, minimal, and welcoming design, carefully structured to guide visitors effortlessly through the property’s story, amenities, and accommodation offerings. Emphasis was placed on visual storytelling, clarity, and ease of navigation to build trust and encourage direct engagement.

Special attention was given to user experience and responsiveness, ensuring the site performs seamlessly across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices. The layout, typography, and color choices reflect a calm and homely aesthetic while maintaining a professional and polished feel.

Key Highlights:

  • Clean, responsive UI optimized for all screen sizes.
  • Intuitive navigation and well-structured content flow.
  • Strong visual emphasis to showcase rooms and ambiance.
  • SEO-friendly structure to improve online visibility.
  • Brand-aligned design that reflects warmth, comfort, and trust.

This project demonstrates my ability to deliver client-focused web solutions, combining design sensibility, technical execution, and business understanding to produce a website that supports brand identity and user engagement.
